About Us

Our mission

LifeTime Paws is dedicated to saving cats and dogs whose end of life is approaching or who have been at the shelter for an extended period of time. No pet should die due to lack of space. Until all shelters become no-kill shelters, LifeTime Paws will do its best to help find homes for perfectly healthy animals that have a chance at having a perfectly happy life with their new forever family.

How to use this website

How you can help

1. Adopt

When you are ready, take the next step in bringing a furry friend into your life. They will change your life with their unconditional love and presence. As one study revealed, "pets are the new kids and plants are the new pets." Look through our list of animals that need their forever home urgently and visit their shelter webiste for detailed adoption process.

2. Donate

LifeTime Paws supports 100% of the adoption fees and first vet examination. In some cases, there would be travel option available for those who would like to adopt from out of state. All of these would not be possible without the financial support from people all around the world. If you would like to make a donation, please contact lifetimepaws@gmail.com.

3. Become a travel companion

Do you love to travel and spend time with cats or dogs? Sign up to become our travel companion for the animals that may find their forever home outside of their city or state. To get started on the approval process, please contact lifetimepaws@gmail.com.
